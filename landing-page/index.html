FeeShare Platform

Including DAO tooling and an EigenLayer AVS, FeeShare automatically updates rewards for smart contract developers based on generated gas fees. It is currently operating on the Swellchain testnet.

Open App Technical Documents

Unlimited Incentive Programs

Create multiple incentive programs for each organization and share revenue with developers.

Verified Rewards

All rewards are verified by multiple operators using EigenLayer AVS, ensuring correctness and security.

DAO Management

Organizations can manage budgets, programs, and rewards through DAO mechanisms with full transparency and decentralization.
